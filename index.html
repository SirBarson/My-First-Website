Welcome to IEC Online Voting System

1. How to register?

Register to vote and exercise your democratic right!

1. Navigate to Registration: Click on "REGISTRATION" in the navigation menu.

2. Select Registration Type: Choose the type of registration you need, such as "New Voter Registration" or "Updating Voter Information".

3. Fill out the Form:ete the registration form with accurate information.

4. Submit the Form: Once you've filled out the form, submit it according to the provided instructions.

5. Confirmation: Await confirmation of your registration. You may receive an email or other communication confirming your registration.

TWO POINT VOTING PROCEDURE

2. How to vote?

Cast your vote responsibly!

1. Navigate to Voting Process:Click on "VOTING PROCESS" in the navigation menu.

2. Learn How to Vote: Read the provided information about the voting process, including any instructions or guidelines.

3. Access the Ballot: Once you're ready to vote, access the online ballot system provided on the website.

4. Make Your Selection: Carefully review the options available and select your preferred candidate or choice.

5. Submit Your Vote: Follow the prompts to submit your vote securely through the online system.

Confirmation: After submitting your vote, look for confirmation that your vote has been received and recorded.
